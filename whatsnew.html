3. Parameters that are being taken into consideration:
  • Data from cricheros/stumps will be used - Number of Matches, Runs Scored, Wickets Taken, and Catches Taken
  • In past have you been a captain or not
  • Are you on time in the matches?
  • Age?
  • Present on the auction day?
  • Rating given by the core committee â€“ Every player will be given a rating by the core committee
Using the above details - Software will calculate a rating by a written logic and that rating will be provided to every player.
The above number will be used to decide the team owner
On the auction day, every one will have to mark their attendance and provide a random number between 0 - 100. The average of all the entries will be used as a benchmark. And the closest players to the above number will be the team owners.
Example
Player X has received a rating of 54 by the above calculation. On the auction day, the random number selected by everyone is 54
Player Rating by the Software
X 52
Y 45
Z 67
On auction day, random number selected was 70.
In this case Player Z will be the TEAM OWNER.
